The difficulty levels break down to the following characteristics:

  • Level 1 - only coins and gems (no stars). Coins are either a 2:1 or 3:1 exchange rate for gems.
  • Level 2 - coins, gems, and stars. Coins are a 2:1 exchange rate for gems and a 3:1 rate for stars.
  • Level 3 - coins, gems, and stars. Coins are either a 2:1 or 3:1 exchange rate to gems, and either a 3:1 or 4:1 rate to stars. Gems and stars will never be worth the same number of coins.
